What does it do?

Secure Co-pilot automatically detects unprofessional or risky text in any text box and suggests improvements. Because it runs entirely on your computer, your data never leaves your device.

How to use it:

  1. Simply start typing in a text area or editable field.
  2. If the extension detects text that could be improved, a üõ°Ô∏è icon will appear.
  3. Hover over the icon to see the AI-powered suggestion.
